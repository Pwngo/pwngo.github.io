Membership Information

At SpaceTime Makerspace, we offer various membership options tailored to fit your needs. Choose the membership tier that best suits you:

Membership Tiers:

  • Basic Membership: Access to shared equipment during open hours.

    • Access to state-of-the-art equipment and tools.
    • Discounts on workshops, classes, and events.
    • Networking opportunities with fellow makers and professionals.

  • Premium Membership: Extended access to equipment and priority registration for workshops.

    • Access to state-of-the-art equipment and tools.

    • Access to state-of-the-art equipment and tools.
    • Discounts on workshops, classes, and events.
    • Networking opportunities with fellow makers and professionals.
    • Exclusive member-only events and projects.
    • Free or discounted access to partner facilities and resources.
    • Discounts on reserving parts of the makerpace for your company

    • Early notification about workshops and other events
